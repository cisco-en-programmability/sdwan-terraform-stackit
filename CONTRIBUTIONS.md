# Contributions

For contributions, questions, or review coordination for this repository, reach out to [ssalemar@cisco.com](mailto:ssalemar@cisco.com).

When proposing changes:

- include the Terraform, cloud-init, and script impact together when they are coupled
- call out any manual portal steps or Smart Account dependencies in the change description
- keep generated artifacts, local certs, Terraform state, and `terraform.tfvars` out of version control
